How it works?

A simple, guided process

1

Log in to your account.

Use your username and password. New here? Create an account and confirm your email — you'll receive a confirmation link right away.

2

Search for your tutorial.

Once you're logged in, use the search box to find your tutorial — for example, type 607.

3

Print the template and watch the tutorial.

Adjust the template to your preferred size, print it, and use it to cut and bake your cookies. Make your royal icing, press play, and follow along. It will feel like I am right there next to you! Now take a moment and look at your beautiful cookies. You made it!
